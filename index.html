About Veve

Who is Veve Zulfikar?

Veve Zulfikar, born Veryal Eisha Aqila Zulfikar Basyaiban on July 7, 2003, in Jombang, East Java, Indonesia, comes from a deeply religious family. Her father, KH. Zulfikar Basyaiban, is a respected Qur'an reciter and Islamic scholar. Her grandfather, Habib Muhammad Rozi Syihab Basyaiban, founded an Islamic boarding school.

Veve began her public career in 2016 through YouTube, sharing Qur'anic recitations, salawat, and Islamic songs. Her content quickly gained popularity for its combination of religious tradition and modern presentation.

Career Timeline

2016 - YouTube Debut

Began her public career through YouTube, sharing Qur'anic recitations, salawat, and Islamic songs.

2017 - Music Debut

Released her first single "Sepercik Do'a Cinta" and made a cameo appearance in the film "Lima Penjuru Masjid".

2018 - Musical Success

Released several successful tracks including "Berakhir Indah", "Sholawat Burdah", and "Sholawat Nahdliyah".

2018 - Santri of The Year

Named "Santri of The Year" for her contributions to Islamic arts and da'wah.

2019 - Film Appearance

Featured in the film "The Santri", expanding her reach in Islamic entertainment.
